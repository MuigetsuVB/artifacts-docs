Artifacts is a sandbox MMORPG designed for code lovers, where you **control your characters entirely through an HTTP API**. Every action your characters can take corresponds to an endpoint you can interact with programmatically. This unique approach lets you **automate gameplay**, **build custom tools**, and **explore new ways to play**.

## Seasons
Artifacts operates on a seasonal server system, with each season lasting approximately 4 months. Each season introduces new features, content, and challenges, giving you the opportunity to be among the first to complete achievements and earn unique badges.

<ServerStatus />

## Gameplay

Artifacts is a **sandbox MMORPG** with **no predetermined path**—you choose your own adventure.
Send your characters into **combat against monsters**, **gather resources** using skills like mining and woodcutting, **craft equipment**, **complete tasks**, and much more.


<Image src={screenshot} alt="screenshot" width={1200} />

The game's **economy is player-driven**: players can sell and buy items on the Grand Exchange.

Explore our [gameplay concepts](/concepts/actions/) for a deeper dive, or jump right in with the [Getting Started guide](/getting-started/).


### Features

- Elemental combat system (fire, air, water, earth) with dynamic stats and progression
- 50 levels of combat and skills with diverse playstyles
- 8 skills: Mining, Woodcutting, Cooking, Alchemy, Gearcrafting, Weaponcrafting, Jewelrycrafting
- Boss fights and unique monsters with challenging mechanics
- Grand Exchange for player-to-player trading with buy/sell offers
- NPCs: merchants and traders for buying, selling, and trading items
- Item crafting and trading with **300+ recipes**
- Banking system for storing and managing items
- Recycling system: recycle crafted equipment to recover resources
- Dynamic world events with exclusive monsters, resources, and special NPCs
- Tasks system for earning gold and task coins with random objectives
- Achievements system with seasonal rankings and account-wide progression
