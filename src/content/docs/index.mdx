## Seasons

Artifacts operates on a **seasonal server system**, with each season lasting approximately **4 months**. Every season resets progress and introduces new features, content, and challenges — giving you the chance to earn unique badges and compete for first completions.

<ServerStatus />

## Gameplay

Artifacts is a sandbox with **no predetermined path** — you choose how to play. Send your characters into **combat against monsters**, **gather resources** with skills like mining and woodcutting, **craft equipment**, **complete tasks**, **trade on the Grand Exchange**, and more.

<Image src={screenshot} alt="Artifacts game screenshot" width={1200} />

### Key Features

- **Elemental combat** — Fire, water, earth, and air with dynamic stats and progression
- **8 skills** — Mining, Woodcutting, Fishing, Alchemy, Cooking, Gearcrafting, Weaponcrafting, Jewelrycrafting
- **300+ craftable items** — Weapons, armor, jewelry, consumables, and resources
- **Grand Exchange** — Player-to-player marketplace with buy and sell orders
- **NPCs** — Merchants and traders for buying, selling, and bartering
- **Boss fights** — Multi-character encounters with up to 3 characters
- **Tasks system** — Random objectives for gold and task coin rewards
- **Achievements** — Seasonal rankings and account-wide progression
- **Dynamic events** — World events with exclusive monsters, resources, and NPCs
- **Banking** — Secure item and gold storage with expandable slots
- **Recycling** — Break down crafted equipment to recover materials
