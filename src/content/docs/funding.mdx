Artifacts is free to play, but operating and developing the game has ongoing costs. The optional Artifacts subscription supports server maintenance and continued development while providing additional features and rewards.

## Subscription

You can view the current plans, prices, renewal details, and manage your subscription from your [member area](https://www.artifactsmmo.com/account/).

The game remains fully playable without a subscription. Member benefits provide testing tools, convenience features, cosmetics, and extra season rewards rather than required gameplay access.

## Founder Program

The Founder Program has ended and has been replaced by the subscription system. Founder titles, badges, and granted skins remain permanently attached to founder accounts.
