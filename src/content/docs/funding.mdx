## End of the Founder Program

At launch, we introduced the **Founders program** a bit hastily, mainly to cover **high server costs**. Since then, we’ve taken a step back and put a lot more thought into the **business model** so it’s simpler, fairer, and more sustainable.


### What’s changing in June 2026?

With the arrival of **the release**, the Founder program will end and be replaced by a traditional subscription.  The subscription is optional to support the game; the game is completely free to play and will remain so.

- **$6/ month**, or  
- **$60/ year** (equivalent to **$5/ month**)

Founders will receive subscription tokens that can be used to continue enjoying their benefits.

| Tier         | Gems  | Subscription Time |
|--------------|-------|-------------------|
| Founder      | 750 | **5 months**      |
| Gold Founder | 1,800 | **12 months**     |
| VIP Founder  | 3,600| **24 months**     |

Founders will receive 50 bonus gems for each month of their subscription. A standard subscription provides 100 gems per month, while founders will receive 150 gems for each month of their subscription.

If you made **multiple purchases**, they will be **combined**.

---

### Founder Titles & Skins

Your **Founder title** (regardless of tier) will be **kept forever** in-game and on Discord and will **no longer be obtainable**.  
A **unique character skin** will also be granted for **each tier** by the end of the Founders program.


### How do I use gems?

Gems can be used to purchase **character skins**, **host spawn events** for the community, or to **extend your subscription**.

Starting in Season 8, all players will be able to get them for free through the new season rewards system.

## Subscription

### Fight Simulator
A complete combat simulator that lets you simulate any combat scenario against a monster or a boss. Run up to **100 iterations per simulation** with full combat logs to accurately assess your chances and optimize your strategy. Available via the API and in-client.

### Season Rewards (Extra Rewards)
Earn **400 additional gems** and an **exclusive skin** by earning achievement points each season.

### WebSocket Access
Real-time notifications via WebSocket for Grand Exchange orders and trades, live event spawns and removals, pending items, announcements, achievement unlocks, and account activity logs.


### Sandbox Server Access
Test freely. Extra endpoints to grant items, XP, and gold, spawn events, and more. Available via the API and in-client (sandbox mode).

### Beta Server (Season Preview)
Early access on a beta server to discover and prepare for the next season before everyone else. Generally available **one to two weeks before the official season launch**. All sandbox features are enabled.

### Account Stats
Complete statistics for your account, accessible on your profile: monsters killed, number of requests, number of deaths, number of items crafted, and more.

### Game Assistant
Ask up to **25 questions per day** to the AI-powered game assistant, capable of concretely explaining any game concept or mechanic - directly in the client or via the API.

### 100 Gems / Month*
Receive a bonus of **100 gems** for each month of your subscription to buy character skins, host spawn events, or extend your subscription.

*This bonus only applies to subscriptions paid with real money, not to subscriptions purchased with gems.
