Artifacts is an API-based MMORPG. Every action your characters can take — moving, fighting, gathering, crafting — corresponds to an HTTP endpoint.

You can play directly using the API by sending HTTP requests from your favorite programming language, or use our official [Python SDK](/sdks/python/introduction) which automatically handles cooldowns, provides typed models, and offers async support.

## Before You Start

The first step is to [create your account](https://www.artifactsmmo.com/account/register) and your first character by logging in. After that you'll need your token, which you can find on [your account page](https://www.artifactsmmo.com/my).

:::caution 
The token is used by the server to identify you when you make requests. It is important to keep it secret. It refreshes when you change your password.
:::

## Quickstart Tutorial

To get started, open our **game client** here: [https://play.artifactsmmo.com](https://play.artifactsmmo.com), and click the **Tutorial** button in the top right corner.

<Image src={tutorial} alt="Tutorial"  />

The tutorial guides you **step by step** through your first movements, combat against your first monster, recovering your hit points, and crafting your first weapon.

It's available in **Python** and **JavaScript** directly in your browser, but you can use any language to follow the tutorial steps by sending the corresponding HTTP requests. You can find examples in other languages in the [API documentation](https://api.artifactsmmo.com/docs/#/).
