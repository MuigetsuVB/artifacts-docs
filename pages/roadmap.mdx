# Roadmap 

**Artifacts** is scheduled to launch in 2026 with its version 1.  
Here is the planned roadmap until the end of the year.

## Planned for Launch:
- New web game client  
- Finalization of the website  
- Update of the Artifacts map to fully integrate the new map system  
- Achievement system update  
- Economy update:  
  - Addition of buy offers in the Grand Exchange  
  - Dynamic pricing for resources sold/bought by NPCs  
- Launch of the subscription system  
- Launch of the permanent test server: **Artifacts Sandbox**  
- Addition of webhooks  

## Future Features (this section will be updated in the future):
- Mount system to move faster on the map  
- Multiplayer combat system (PVP, PVE)
